Software Form 1042-S - Withholding Agent - Payer :

A Withholding Agent - Payer is a US entity, which can be an Individual, Corporation, University or other entity, that pays US source income to a Foreign Person or Recipient.

A withholding Agent must report Tax Treaty based Income paid to a Foreign person on Form 1042-S, and withhold Federal Taxes based on the right tax rate, before making the payment.
