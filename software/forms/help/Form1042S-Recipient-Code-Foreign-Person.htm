Software Form 1042-S - Recipient Code - Foreign Person :

A Foreign person is a Non Resident Alien, to whom a Tax Treaty based Income is paid.

A Foreign Person who does not meet the Green card Test, Substantial Presence or under exempt status in F, J, M Visas,
is considered a Non Resident Aliens. Also Bona Fide residents of Puerto Rico, Guam, American Samoa, the Commonwealth
of the Northern Marinara Islands are considered as Non Resident Aliens.

Recipient Type :

Individual is the recipient type of a Non Resident Alien.

Recipient Code:

The Recipient Code for an Individual is 01.
